I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at <a href="https://www.vt.edu/">Virginia Tech</a>, mentored by <a href="https://shaddih.com/">Professor Dr. Shaddi Hasan</a>. My research focuses on <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Internet Measurement</span>, <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Broadband Internet Policy</span>, and <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Internet Data Analytics</span>, addressing real-world challenges in <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Computer Networks</span> and <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Information and Communication Technologies for Development (ICTD)</span>. I study how Internet infrastructure and routing affect <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">end-user connectivity and Quality of Experience (QoE)</span>, using large-scale measurement data to understand network performance and accessibility.

My expertise combines <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Statistical Methodologies</span> with <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Machine Learning</span> to analyze large-scale Internet measurement datasets. My research presented at <a href="https://conferences.sigcomm.org/imc/2024/">ACM IMC 2024</a> — the <em>Broadband Integrity</em> project (paper: <em>“Red is Sus”</em>) — analyzes measurement data to identify discrepancies in the <a href="https://broadbandmap.fcc.gov/">U.S. National Broadband Map</a>. This work develops automated methods to detect questionable service availability claims by ISPs and generate insights that support <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Broadband Transparency</span> and <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Broadband Equity</span>. In 2026, I was selected as an <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Internet Society Pulse Fellow</span> under the <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Internet Resilience</span> category.

Building on this work, I am developing <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">NetBreakout</span>, a measurement-driven approach for mapping mobile network egress topology using large-scale traceroute data. I am also interested in <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">IP Geolocation Validation</span>, using network measurements and topology analysis to evaluate and improve the accuracy of geolocation databases widely used by Internet services and measurement platforms.

My prior experience as a <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Network Planner</span> provides practical insight into broadband and cellular infrastructure, helping me bridge the gap between <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">network measurement research</span> and real-world network operations.

Through my work, I aim to develop measurement methods and data-driven tools that help researchers, network operators, and policymakers better understand Internet infrastructure and improve connectivity, performance, and <span style="color:MediumSeaGreen">Digital Equity</span>.
